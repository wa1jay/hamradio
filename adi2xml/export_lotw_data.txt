
To export all of you lotw data in adi format:

	1.Replace CALLSIGN in this url to your callsign.
	2. Login to lotw on your browser.
	3. Open another browser tab and paste the url into the address bar, it will churn for a minute then you should get a download of your lotw data.

https://lotw.arrl.org/lotwuser/lotwreport.adi?qso_query=1&login=CALLSIGN&qso_qsl=yes&qso_qsldetail=yes&qso_mydetail=yes&qso_withown=yes&qso_qslsince=1900-01-01&qso_qsorxsince=1900-01-01
